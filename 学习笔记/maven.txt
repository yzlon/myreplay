pom.xml：


Maven Update Project之后jdk总是变成1.5，只要在pom.xml中增加如下插件即可
	<build>
		<plugins>
			<plugin>
				<groupId>org.apache.maven.plugins</groupId>
				<artifactId>maven-compiler-plugin</artifactId>
				<version>3.1</version>
				<configuration>
					<source>1.6</source>
					<target>1.6</target>
					<showWarnings>true</showWarnings>
				</configuration>
			</plugin>
		</plugins>
	</build>
